XXL - Lany

Lany is an American pop rock band from New York City, formed in 2014. The band consists of lead vocalist and guitarist Jake Bundrick and bassist and backing vocalist Alex Gaskarth. They are known for their melodic sound and emotionally charged lyrics.

Lany gained recognition with their debut album, "Malibu," released in 2016. The album featured hit singles like "Ain't Your Mama" and "Ain't Your Mama (Reprise)," which helped establish the band as a rising force in the pop rock scene. Their second album, "The Endless River," released in 2018, continued their success with tracks that showcased their growth as musicians.

In addition to their studio albums, Lany has also released several EPs and singles, further solidifying their presence in the music industry. Their music often explores themes of love, heartbreak, and self-discovery, resonating with a wide audience.
